Tic Tac Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe, gives traces of being invented in Egypt around 1300BCE, a game of Circles and Crosses, or Xs and Os is a 'Paper-Pencil' game for two players who take turns by marking the empty spaces in a three-by-three grid with O or X. The player who is able to place three of their marks(X | O) in a Horizontal, Vertical, or Diagonal Row gets the title of WINNER. Here you can play Tic-Tac-Toe either with computer or with your friend. This site has the simplest and most understanding designs so it is easy to understand the game!
